Sponsoring a Final Project

In order to sponsor a final project the below form must be filled out. Your sponsorship will require you to meet occasionally with the team you sponsor as well as provide them with any information needed to understand the project.
After filling out the form your request will be processed by the Cvil Engineering Department to determine whether it is suitable for our students to have as their final project. We will contact you with our decision and if deemed suitable, facilatate a meeting for further planning.
